Privacy Policy

We collect personal information such as your name, contact details, and pet information through our booking forms. This information helps us provide grooming services and manage appointments effectively.

We collect this information directly from you and do not share it with third parties. You have the right to view or correct your information at any time by contacting us.

Providing your information is voluntary, but if you choose not to, we may not be able to offer certain services such as online bookings.

To contact us, email admin@snazzypaws.co.nz.
